Working of the Metaverse

Metaverse-infused education platform which offers a variety of courses and programs, covering a wide range of subjects allowing learners to explore, discover, and create in a virtual environment.

Services

A virtual space where users can interact with each other and with digital objects in a shared environment. It's a concept that's been around for decades, but recent advancements in technology have made it more accessible and immersive than ever before.

VR Classroom

Virtual classroom is a dynamic and interactive environment designed to provide you with an engaging and immersive learning experience. Using the latest virtual reality technology, we have created a classroom that feels just like a physical classroom, but with all the benefits of being online.

  • Fosters interaction and collaboration among students and instructors.
  • More cost-effective than traditional offline education.

Monument Venture

You can travel to some of the most iconic locations on the planet and witness them in a way that's never been possible before. Using cutting-edge virtual reality technology, our tour allows you to immerse yourself in these landmarks, providing a level of detail and interactivity that is truly breathtaking.

  • Visit Historical monuments virtually
  • Best possible experience

AR Learning

Students can explore a virtual world that brings their educational content to life. Using the AR technology, students can scan objects or images to trigger interactive 3D models, animations, and simulations that provide a deeper understanding of the concepts being taught.

  • Best augmented experience
  • Experience lab equipments in your home
  • Experience difficult biology and chemistry structures

Disaster Xperience

One potential use case for the metaverse could be simulating disaster experiences to help people prepare for and respond to real-life disasters. In the metaverse this could be a valuable tool for preparing individuals and communities for disasters and promoting safety and resilience.

  • Preparedness Training
  • Realistic Experience
  • Simulation Environment
